about us

Aegean Estiatorio is a traditional Greek restaurant with a modern sensibility.

At Aegean Estiatorio, we believe in letting the integrity of our ingredients speak for itself. Freshness, simplicity, quality, and tradition are our core kitchen values. We strive to offer the highest-quality fresh seafood possible.

Everything on our menu has been prepared fresh daily, honoring traditional preparations with an eye for detail. Come join us and experience the essence of Greek cuisine.
